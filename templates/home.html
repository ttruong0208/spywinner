Frequently asked questions

Scan finished but no products showed up?

Facebook ads are still captured (see raw_ads.csv). The Strict winner filter with broad keywords (e.g. "cleaning") may return 0 qualifying products — that's normal. Use the Balanced preset or review the scored table in your job.

How do I pay for Pro/VIP?

Pay in USD via PayPal, Wise, USDT, or our checkout link — include your payment reference. We activate within 24 hours. No hidden card charges.

How is this different from AdSpy / Minea / BigSpy?

They offer massive ad archives and multiple platforms (Minea includes Pinterest), typically $50–$149/month. WinnerSpy focuses on keyword-driven Facebook scans, product scoring, TikTok/Trends on higher plans, and affordable pricing — see the comparison table above.

Does it estimate shop revenue like WinningHunter?

Not yet. We focus on ad signals (volume, campaign longevity, creative). Shop revenue estimates may come in a future release.

Do you guarantee bestsellers?

No. WinnerSpy surfaces signals from real ads — you still need to test products and creatives yourself.

Do I need to install software?

No. Just a browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari…) and a WinnerSpy account.

How long does a scan take?

Usually 5–15 minutes depending on volume. Track progress live in the web app.

Why not just browse Ads Library myself?

We aggregate products, score them, and export reports — saving hours every week.
